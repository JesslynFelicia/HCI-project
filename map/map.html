WELCOME TO MAP PAGE




Rimaro

This map is the map most frequently used map by LaForant players. Because of its simple design and not hard to memorize, many new players choose to play on this map

Dynamite

This map is more difficult than the first map, because the area is wider and there are many places for players to hide which can eventually surprise other players who are passing by. However, this map is still widely used by players who are usually level 20 and above, because players with level 20 and above already have the ability to play on this map, but this map is not recommended for new players

Daydream

This map is the most difficult map among all the maps in LaForant. Not only is the place spacious, there are also many teleportation areas that can make players who pass through the area move directly to other teleportation areas. Usually players who play using this map are players who have played for a long time and already have a high rank, so they already have a good ability to fight each other in this map.
